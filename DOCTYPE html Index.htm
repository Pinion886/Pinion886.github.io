<section id="problem" class="problem">

<div class="container">

<h2>The Problem We're Solving</h2>

<div class="problem-content">

<p class="problem-highlight">UK trade professionals and businesses have no reliable way to assess customer risk before accepting work.</p>

<div class="problem-details">

<div class="problem-column">

<h3>For Construction</h3>

<p>Construction firms are owed 22.6 billion in late payments, with the average firm waiting 107 days for payment.</p>

<p>71% of tradespeople have been victims of non-payment, with an average annual loss of 2,472 per tradesperson.</p>

<p class="source">Source: Federation of Master Builders, Build UK</p>

</div>

<div class="problem-column">

<h3>For Vehicle Traders</h3>

<p>Vehicle-related complaints increased by 17% in 2022, with customer disputes costing dealers an average of 1,890 per case.</p>

<p>37% of vehicle dealers report that better customer information would have prevented costly disputes.</p>

<p class="source">Source: Motor Ombudsman, NFDA</p>

</div>

<div class="problem-column">

<h3>For Suppliers</h3>

<p>Commercial suppliers wait an average of 60 days for payment, with 74% reporting challenges with specific problematic customers.</p>

<p>Small UK wholesalers write off an average of 10,000 per year in bad debts due to inadequate customer information.</p>

<p class="source">Source: CICM UK, Department for Business and Trade</p>

</div>

</div>

</div>

</div>

</section>
